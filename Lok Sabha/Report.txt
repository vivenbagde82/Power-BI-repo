Lok Sabha Detailed Report

Highlights:

The total number of female parliamentarians, which stands at 14.67% or 82/559, is abysmally low.

	This low female representation reflects over every other data point:
		The private bills introduced is lower than the average by 52%.
		Average debate attendance is 18% less than the average.
		A handful of 15 political parties represent all the female parliamentarians.

An initial bump of debate attended and then a continuous decile is observed over the number of times a parliamentarian is elected.
